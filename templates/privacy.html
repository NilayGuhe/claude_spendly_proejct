Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. Please read it carefully.

1. Data We Collect

When you create a Spendly account, we collect basic account information such as your name and email address, along with the password you choose (stored in a hashed form).

To provide expense tracking, we store the financial entries you create — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions — tied to your account. We do not collect your bank credentials or link to your financial accounts.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data you enter is used to operate Spendly: to display your expenses, generate summaries and category breakdowns, and let you filter your spending by date range.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We use your data only to deliver and improve the service you signed up for.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers and is associated only with your account. We take reasonable precautions — including hashed passwords and access controls — to protect your information from unauthorized access.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. By using Spendly, you acknowledge and accept this inherent risk. Please use a strong, unique password and keep your login credentials confidential.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small set of trusted third-party services to operate — for example, hosting infrastructure and standard web analytics. These providers receive only the minimum information needed to perform their function and are bound by their own privacy obligations.

We do not embed advertising trackers and we do not share your expense entries with third parties.

5. Your Choices

You can review, update, or delete the expenses stored in your account at any time from within Spendly. You may also delete your account entirely; when you do, we will remove your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it for legal or security reasons.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the service or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of Spendly after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the service.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the support channels listed in the app.
